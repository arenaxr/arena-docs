Rotation
========


3D object rotation in degrees by default; Right-handed coordinate system. Switches to quaternion representation if 'w' is given

Rotation Attributes
--------------------

|Attribute|Type|Default|Description|Required|
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
|w|number|```1```|w|No|
|x|number|```0```|x|Yes|
|y|number|```0```|y|Yes|
|z|number|```0```|z|Yes|
