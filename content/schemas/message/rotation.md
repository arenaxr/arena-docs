Rotation
========


3D object rotation in degrees by default; Right-handed coordinate system. Switches to quaternion representation if 'w' is given

Rotation Attributes
--------------------

|Attribute|Description|Type|Default|Required|
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
|w|w|number|```1```|No|
|x|x|number|```0```|Yes|
|y|y|number|```0```|Yes|
|z|z|number|```0```|Yes|
