# Rendering Support

The ARENA MQTT and Persistence system of communication and storage support entities and components for rendering and interaction across three client platforms. Each platform's REQUIREMENTS.md is the **source of truth** for support status and planning.

## Supported Entities & Components by Platform

| Platform | Entities | Components | Changelog |
|----------|----------|------------|-----------|
| **Web** (arena-web-core) | [Entities](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-web-core/blob/master/REQUIREMENTS.md#supported-entities) | [Components](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-web-core/blob/master/REQUIREMENTS.md#supported-components) | [Changelog](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-web-core/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md) |
| **Python** (arena-py) | [Entities](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-py/blob/master/REQUIREMENTS.md#supported-entities) | [Components](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-py/blob/master/REQUIREMENTS.md#supported-components) | [Changelog](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-py/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md) |
| **Unity** (arena-unity) | [Entities](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-unity/blob/main/REQUIREMENTS.md#supported-entities) | [Components](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-unity/blob/main/REQUIREMENTS.md#supported-components) | [Changelog](https://github.com/arenaxr/arena-unity/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md) |
